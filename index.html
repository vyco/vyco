PRODUCTION PROCESS

Below is the process that we take for every project that is given to us, all of these steps have proven to work, and produce what we call digital excellence.

WIREFRAMING1

For the first day of creation we will digitally draw the entire website head to toe to see how it will look and feel

brainstorm2

After this we will think more about how the website will look and feel and reasearch other websites to review current web trends to see if these can be incorparated into our clients site

Development3

During this stage we will being to create the bone structure of the site in HTML5 during this course we will design the layout and all of the cotent writing.

Styling4

This is where the fun comes in, here we will customise the website and display our creative flair and talent, as we will style the website to our clients order with their own custom animations and designs.

Scripting5

Towards the end of the process we will crete the backend of the site dealing with all of the information and the ordering of the site using javacsript.

deployment 6

In this final stage we will be looking the website and attemptong to use it through the eyes of a new user. After it has passed our tests we will upload the site!
